# JTLD Consulting Logo - Design System

## Logo Overview

The JTLD Consulting logo represents the core essence of our business consulting services: **growth, connectivity, innovation, and strategic transformation**.

---

### Primary Files
- **`logo.svg`** — Full logo with text (Light background, primary use)

## Design Elements

### Color Palette

| Color Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|-----------|-----|-----|-------|
| **Primary Blue** | #1F3A93 | 31, 58, 147 | Trust, professionalism, stability |
| **Accent Cyan** | #00A8E8 | 0, 168, 232 | Growth, innovation, technology |

- **Logo Text:** Bold, modern sans-serif (Arial/Helvetica alternative)
- **Size:** 18px (JTLD), 10px (CONSULTING)
- **Letter Spacing:** Standard (CONSULTING has increased spacing for emphasis)

## Logo Symbolism

### Visual Components

1. **Three Connected Nodes**
   - Represents **connectivity, collaboration, and strategic partnerships**
   - Left node: Business stakeholders
   - Center node: Core consulting value
   - Right node: Technology enablement

2. **Upward Arrow**
   - Symbolizes **growth, improvement, and positive outcomes**
   - Represents strategic transformation and upward momentum
   - Aligned with our mission to accelerate returns on investments

3. **Connecting Lines**
   - Show **integration across business and technology**
   - Represent data flow, communication, and intelligent automation
   - Emphasize our AI and data analytics services

4. **Decorative Dots**
   - Subtle AI/data science elements
   - Represent intelligent insights and analytics
   - Add visual sophistication and tech credibility

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Light Background (Primary Use)
- **Background:** White or light gray (#F8F9FA)
- **Primary nodes/lines:** #1F3A93 (Primary Blue)
- **Accent/Arrow:** #00A8E8 (Cyan)
- **Best for:** Website, documents, presentations

### Dark Background (Alternative)
- **Background:** #1F3A93 (Primary Blue)
- **Accent nodes/lines:** #00A8E8 (Cyan)
- **Arrow/highlight:** #FFD500 (Gold)
- **Text:** White
- **Best for:** Dark mode, dark banners, video overlays

---

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's ✅
- Use logo on clean, uncluttered backgrounds
- Maintain minimum padding around logo
- Use appropriate color contrast for legibility
- Scale proportionally (maintain aspect ratio)
- Use on white, light gray, or dark backgrounds

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't distort or skew the logo
- Don't add drop shadows or effects (unless branded design allows)
- Don't use colors other than specified palette
- Don't place on busy/patterned backgrounds
- Don't rotate or flip the logo

---

## Sizing Guidelines

- **Website Header:** 150-200px
