# JTLD Consulting Inc — Persona List

## 1. Prospective Clients

### 1.1 The Executive Decision-Maker
- **Role:** C-suite (CEO, COO, CFO, CTO, CIO)
- **Company Size:** Mid-market to enterprise (200–5,000+ employees)
- **Industries:** Financial Services, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Energy, Government, Retail, Telecom, Education
- **Goals:** Drive strategic growth, reduce operational costs, modernize technology, manage risk
- **Pain Points:** Lack of internal expertise for transformation initiatives, pressure from board/stakeholders to show ROI, difficulty hiring specialized talent
- **Platform Behavior:** Visits home page, reads About and Services, checks Client Stories, books a consultation
- **Key Pages:** Home, Services, About, Consultation

### 1.2 The IT / Digital Leader
- **Role:** VP of IT, Director of Digital Transformation, Head of Engineering
- **Company Size:** 100–5,000+ employees
- **Industries:** All 8 verticals
- **Goals:** Modernize infrastructure, adopt cloud/AI, improve security posture, reduce IT spend
- **Pain Points:** Legacy systems, talent gaps, budget constraints, vendor sprawl
- **Platform Behavior:** Dives into specific service pages (Managed IT, Cloud & Hybrid IT, AI), evaluates capabilities, downloads insights
- **Key Pages:** Services (detail pages), Insights, Consultation

### 1.3 The Operations / Process Leader
- **Role:** VP of Operations, Director of Process Excellence, Head of Supply Chain
- **Company Size:** 200–5,000+ employees
- **Industries:** Manufacturing, Retail, Healthcare, Financial Services
- **Goals:** Streamline workflows, reduce waste, improve quality, scale operations
- **Pain Points:** Inefficient manual processes, lack of visibility into operations, siloed data
- **Platform Behavior:** Reads Business Process Services and Data Analytics pages, looks for case studies
- **Key Pages:** Services (Business Process, Data Analytics), Insights, Consultation

### 1.4 The Data / Analytics Leader
- **Role:** Chief Data Officer, VP of Analytics, Head of BI
- **Company Size:** 200–5,000+ employees
- **Industries:** Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail, Telecom
- **Goals:** Build data-driven culture, implement BI/dashboards, enable predictive analytics
- **Pain Points:** Poor data quality, lack of governance, disconnected tools, talent shortage
- **Platform Behavior:** Reads Data Analytics and AI service pages, checks team credentials on About
- **Key Pages:** Services (Data Analytics, AI), About, Consultation

### 1.5 The Small Business Owner
- **Role:** Founder, Owner, Managing Director
- **Company Size:** 10–200 employees
- **Industries:** Any
- **Goals:** Grow the business, professionalize operations, adopt technology cost-effectively
- **Pain Points:** Limited budget, wearing too many hats, unsure where to start with consulting
- **Platform Behavior:** Reads Home and Services overview, uses Tosh chatbot for quick answers, may book consultation
- **Key Pages:** Home, Services, ChatBot (Tosh), Consultation

---

## 2. Job Seekers / Candidates

### 2.1 The Experienced Consultant
- **Role:** Senior Consultant, Manager, Principal — looking to join or switch firms
- **Experience:** 5–15+ years in consulting, Big Four, or industry
- **Goals:** Work on meaningful engagements, career growth, competitive comp, work-life balance
- **Pain Points:** Burnout at large firms, lack of ownership, limited exposure to new technologies
- **Platform Behavior:** Reads Careers page, reviews open positions, checks About for culture/values, submits resume
- **Key Pages:** Careers, About, Resume Form (#apply)

### 2.2 The Early-Career Professional
- **Role:** Analyst, Junior Consultant, Recent Graduate
- **Experience:** 0–4 years
- **Goals:** Break into consulting, learn from senior mentors, build a portfolio of engagements
- **Pain Points:** Hard to get noticed, unclear career paths, limited practical experience
- **Platform Behavior:** Browses Careers for entry-level roles, reads perks section, submits general application
- **Key Pages:** Careers, About, Resume Form (#apply)

### 2.3 The Technical Specialist
- **Role:** Data Engineer, ML Engineer, Cloud Architect, DevOps Engineer
- **Experience:** 3–10+ years
- **Goals:** Work on diverse technical challenges, avoid monotony, stay current with technology
- **Pain Points:** Pigeon-holed at current employer, limited exposure to different industries
- **Platform Behavior:** Reads specific service pages (AI, Cloud, Data Analytics), checks Careers for technical roles
- **Key Pages:** Services (AI, Cloud, Data Analytics), Careers, Resume Form (#apply)

---

## 3. Existing Clients (Portal Users)

### 3.1 The Client Project Sponsor
- **Role:** VP or Director who signed the engagement
- **Goals:** Track project progress, review deliverables, manage budget, communicate with JTLD team
- **Pain Points:** Lack of visibility into project status, difficulty coordinating across teams
- **Platform Behavior:** Logs in via Client Portal, views dashboards, reviews documents
- **Key Pages:** Login, Client Portal (future)

### 3.2 The Client Team Member
- **Role:** Business analyst, IT staff, or operator working alongside JTLD consultants
- **Goals:** Collaborate on deliverables, access shared resources, provide feedback
- **Pain Points:** Tool fragmentation, unclear responsibilities, communication gaps
- **Platform Behavior:** Logs in for shared workspaces and documents
- **Key Pages:** Login, Client Portal (future)

---

## 4. Internal JTLD Staff

### 4.1 The JTLD Consultant
- **Role:** Consultant at any level (Analyst through Partner)
- **Goals:** Access client materials, update engagement status, manage time tracking
- **Platform Behavior:** Logs in to internal tools, accesses knowledge base
- **Key Pages:** Login, Internal Dashboard (future)

### 4.2 The JTLD Talent / HR Manager
- **Role:** Recruiter, Talent Acquisition Lead, HR
- **Goals:** Review incoming applications, screen candidates, manage pipeline
- **Platform Behavior:** Reviews resume submissions from /api/careers/apply, manages candidate pipeline
- **Key Pages:** Login, Admin Dashboard (future), Resume submissions

### 4.3 The JTLD Marketing / Content Manager
- **Role:** Marketing Manager, Content Strategist
- **Goals:** Publish insights/articles, manage newsletter subscribers, track site analytics
- **Platform Behavior:** Publishes to Insights page, manages newsletter, reviews GA4 metrics
- **Key Pages:** Login, CMS (future), Insights
